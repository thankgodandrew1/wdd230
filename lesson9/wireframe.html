Wireframes for Directory Page

Grid View

Below is the grid wireframe for the chamber directory page. Cards on large screens appear on three (3) columns, on medium screen devices, cards appear on two columns while on small screen or small phones cards appear on a single column. Each card has the following contents!

  • Image/logo of a Business
  • Business Name
  • Business Address
  • Phone Number
  • Business Membership Level and
  • Business Website URL

List View

Below is the list wireframe for the chamber directory page. Cards on the list view are to be displayed in a list format once a client clicks on the list button, the list view posseses contents same as the grid view although the business image/logo content will be limited on medium and small screens, but will be available on large screens.
Contents on large and medium screens will be displayed in rows while contents on small screens or small phones will be displayed in columns
