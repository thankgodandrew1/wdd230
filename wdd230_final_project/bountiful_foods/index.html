About Carlsbad, California

Carlsbad is a coastal city in California with a population of 114,746. Carlsbad is in San Diego County and is one of the best places to live in California.

Living in Carlsbad offers residents a dense suburban feel and most residents own their homes, Carlsbad is also a popular tourist destination.
